Introducing Cargi

Cargi is your personalized driving assistant. Cargi integrates with your Calendar, Contacts, Navigation, and Music to provide a seamless driving experience where everything is accessible with one touch from a dashboard.

About Cargi

You have dinner with your friend at a steakhouse scheduled for 6:30 on your Calendar. You get into your car and open up Cargi. Cargi automatically recognizes your event and starts navigating you to dinner. Cargi also places the contact information of your friend on the dashboard, so you can keep them updated on your ETA.

You get stuck in traffic and want to stay entertained. Cargi allows you to open up your favorite Music app with a single tap. Finally, you’re almost at dinner when you realize you are running low on gas. You tap Cargi’s gas button and the nearest and cheapest gas stations are immediately visible to you, with the gas prices at each station.
